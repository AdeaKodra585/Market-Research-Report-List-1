<p><strong>The Silicon Wafer Cleaning Chemicals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Cleaning</li><li>Hydrofluoric Acid Dip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicon wafer cleaning chemicals market encompasses various methods, primarily focusing on solvent cleaning and hydrofluoric acid dip. Solvent cleaning uses organic solvents to remove organic contaminants and residues from the wafer surface, ensuring high purity levels. In contrast, hydrofluoric acid dip specifically targets the removal of native silicon dioxide layers and other inorganic impurities, making it critical for optimal wafer performance. Both methods are essential in semiconductor manufacturing, contributing to the quality and efficiency of silicon wafer production.</p></p>
