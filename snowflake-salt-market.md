<p><strong>The Snowflake Salt Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Granule</li><li>Lamellate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Snowflake salt is classified into two primary market types: granule and lamellate. Granule snowflake salt consists of larger, irregularly shaped crystals that provide a coarse texture, commonly used in culinary applications and for seasoning. In contrast, lamellate snowflake salt features thinner, flat, and layered crystals that dissolve quickly, making it ideal for finishing dishes and adding a delicate touch. Both types cater to different culinary needs, enhancing flavors and presentations in various dishes.</p></p>
