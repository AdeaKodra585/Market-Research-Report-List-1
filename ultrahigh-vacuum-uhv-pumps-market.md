<p><ul><li>Ion Getter Pumps (IGPs)</li><li>Titanium Sublimator Pumps (TSPs)</li><li>Non-evaporable Getters (NEG) Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrahigh vacuum (UHV) pumps market comprises various types, including Ion Getter Pumps (IGPs), Titanium Sublimator Pumps (TSPs), and Non-evaporable Getter (NEG) Pumps. IGPs utilize ionization to trap gases, ensuring low pressure. TSPs employ the sublimation of titanium to create a vacuum environment. NEG pumps are designed to adsorb gas molecules without the need for regeneration, operating efficiently at lower temperatures. Together, these technologies play crucial roles in scientific and industrial applications requiring extreme vacuum conditions.</p></p>
