<p><strong>The Smart Gas Meter System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AMR</li><li>AMI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Gas Meter System market primarily encompasses two types: Automatic Meter Reading (AMR) and Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI). AMR involves the automated collection of gas consumption data, enabling utility companies to streamline billing and monitoring without manual readings. In contrast, AMI offers a more sophisticated approach, integrating two-way communication between the meter and the utility provider, facilitating real-time data exchange, enhanced energy management, and improved customer engagement. Both systems aim to increase efficiency and accuracy in gas consumption tracking.</p></p>
