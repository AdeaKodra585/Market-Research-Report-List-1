<p><p>Automated Peritoneal Dialysis (APD) machines are essential for patients requiring renal support, with two primary market types: power type and gravity type. Power-type machines use electrical pumps to facilitate fluid movement, ensuring consistent and efficient dialysis cycles. In contrast, gravity-type machines rely on gravitational force to manage fluid flow, making them simpler and often more cost-effective. Both types cater to varying patient needs and preferences, influencing their adoption rates and market dynamics within the healthcare sector.</p></p>
