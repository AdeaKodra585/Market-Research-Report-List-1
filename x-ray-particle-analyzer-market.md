<p><strong>The X-ray Particle Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-ray Diffraction Type</li><li>X-ray Fluorescence Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-ray particle analyzer market comprises two primary types: X-ray Diffraction (XRD) and X-ray Fluorescence (XRF). XRD is utilized for identifying crystalline materials and determining their structural properties by analyzing the diffraction patterns of X-rays passing through a sample. Conversely, XRF is used for elemental analysis, providing insights into the composition of materials by measuring the characteristic fluorescent X-rays emitted after excitation. Both techniques are vital in fields such as materials science, geology, and quality control in various industries.</p></p>
