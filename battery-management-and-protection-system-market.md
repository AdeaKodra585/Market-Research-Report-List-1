<p><p>The Battery Management and Protection System (BMPS) market can be divided into decentralized and centralized types. Decentralized systems manage individual battery cells or modules independently, enhancing safety and performance by quickly responding to failures at the cell level. In contrast, centralized systems monitor and control the entire battery pack from a single unit, simplifying design and integration but potentially posing risks if a single point of failure occurs. Both types aim to optimize battery life and ensure safe operation in various applications.</p></p>
