<p><strong>The Geothermal Heat Pump Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Closed Loop</li><li>Horizontal Closed Loop</li><li>Open Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Geothermal heat pump systems utilize the earth's constant temperature for heating and cooling. The market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Vertical Closed Loop**: Buried deep in the ground, these systems use long vertical pipes to circulate fluid, making them ideal for small lots. </p><p>2. **Horizontal Closed Loop**: This type involves buried pipes laid horizontally in trenches, suitable for larger areas. </p><p>3. **Open Loop**: Utilizing groundwater from wells or aquifers, this system circulates water directly, providing efficient heating and cooling when water is abundant.</p></p>
