<p><ul><li>Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI)</li><li>Mechanical. Fuel Injection System (MFI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fuel injection systems market primarily consists of two types: Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI) and Mechanical Fuel Injection (MFI). EFI utilizes electronic controls to optimize fuel delivery, ensuring precise air-fuel mixture for improved performance and efficiency. It offers benefits like reduced emissions and enhanced power. In contrast, MFI relies on mechanical components to inject fuel, traditionally used in older engines with simpler designs. While MFI is generally less efficient, it remains popular in specific applications for its reliability and lower costs.</p></p>
