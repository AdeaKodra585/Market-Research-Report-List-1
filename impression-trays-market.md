<p><p>The Impression Trays market is divided into two primary types: those designed for partial dentures and those for complete dentures. Impression trays for partial dentures are tailored to capture specific areas of the mouth, accommodating remaining natural teeth and supporting soft tissues. In contrast, trays for complete dentures are larger, designed to encompass the entire arch for a comprehensive impression. Both types are crucial for accurate dental fittings, ensuring optimal comfort and functionality of dentures for patients.</p></p>
