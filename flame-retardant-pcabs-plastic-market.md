<p><p>The Flame Retardant PC/ABS Plastic Market is predominantly divided into two key types based on processing methods: Injection Molding and Extrusion Molding. Injection Molding involves shaping molten plastic by injecting it into a mold, ideal for creating complex parts with precision. In contrast, Extrusion Molding involves forcing heated plastic through a die to produce continuous shapes like sheets or profiles, suitable for larger volumes. Both methods cater to diverse applications, including automotive, electronics, and construction, where flame resistance is crucial.</p></p>
