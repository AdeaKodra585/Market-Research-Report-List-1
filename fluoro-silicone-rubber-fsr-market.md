<p><p>Fluoro Silicone Rubber (FSR) is a specialized elastomer known for its exceptional resistance to extreme temperatures, chemicals, and various environmental factors. The market is segmented into two main types: High Temperature Vulcanization (HTV) and Room Temperature Vulcanization (RTV). HTV FSR is cured using heat, providing superior mechanical properties and high-performance applications, while RTV FSR cures at room temperature, offering convenience and ease of use for a variety of applications, including seals, gaskets, and automotive components.</p></p>
