<p><strong>The Steel Industry Refractory Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brick Refractories</li><li>Monolithic Refractories</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel industry refractory material market is primarily categorized into two types: brick refractories and monolithic refractories. Brick refractories consist of pre-formed, fired bricks that provide high thermal stability and durability, making them suitable for lining furnaces and kilns. In contrast, monolithic refractories are unshaped materials, such as castables and plastics, that can be molded into various shapes and are ideal for applications requiring seamless linings. Both types are essential for enhancing the efficiency and longevity of steel production processes.</p></p>
