<p><p>The Smart Card OEM/ODM market encompasses technologies that enhance security through One-Time Password (OTP) and Multifactor Authentication (MFA) solutions. OTP provides a unique, single-use code for user verification, minimizing the risk of unauthorized access. In contrast, MFA combines multiple verification methods, such as passwords, biometrics, or OTPs, to bolster security. Both market types are crucial for protecting sensitive data in various sectors, including finance, healthcare, and government, driving demand for advanced smart card technologies.</p></p>
