<p><p>The BR rubber compound market comprises primarily two types: Cold-Polymerized Isobutylene-Isoprene Rubber (CIIR) and Bromo-Butyl Isoprene Rubber (BIIR). CIIR offers excellent resistance to aging, heat, and weathering, making it suitable for various applications, including automotive inner tubes and seals. BIIR, on the other hand, is known for its superior impermeability and elasticity, often used in tire manufacturing and other high-performance applications. These compounds are essential in enhancing the durability and performance of rubber products across industries.</p></p>
