<p><p>The automotive IGBT market comprises various types, primarily focusing on CIB (Converter Inverter Bridge), PIM (Power Integration Module), and IPM (Intelligent Power Module). CIB serves as a compact solution for power conversion in electric vehicle drives, while PIM combines multiple power components for enhanced efficiency and space reduction. IPM integrates protection and control functions, increasing reliability and simplifying design. Each type plays a crucial role in improving the performance and efficiency of electric and hybrid vehicles.</p></p>
