<p><strong>The Battery Management System (BMS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Distributed</li><li>Centralized</li><li>Modular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A Battery Management System (BMS) monitors and manages battery performance, ensuring safety and longevity. In the market, there are three main types: </p><p>1. **Distributed BMS** allocates control and monitoring to individual battery modules, enhancing flexibility and fault tolerance. </p><p>2. **Centralized BMS** uses a single control unit for multiple batteries, reducing complexity but limiting scalability. </p><p>3. **Modular BMS** combines aspects of both, allowing scalability through interconnected modules while maintaining centralized oversight for efficiency. Each type caters to different applications and technologies.</p></p>
