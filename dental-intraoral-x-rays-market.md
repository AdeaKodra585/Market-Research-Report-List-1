<p><ul><li>Bitewing X-rays</li><li>Periapical X-rays</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental intraoral X-ray market comprises several types, primarily including bitewing and periapical X-rays. Bitewing X-rays are used to detect dental issues in the upper and lower teeth simultaneously, often revealing cavities between teeth. Periapical X-rays focus on a specific tooth or area, capturing the entire tooth from crown to root, useful for diagnosing issues below the gum line. Other types include occlusal and panoramic X-rays, providing comprehensive views for various dental assessments and treatments.</p></p>
