<p><p>The Rotating Biological Contactors (RBC) market comprises two primary types: anaerobic and aerobic RBCs. Anaerobic rotating biological contactors operate without oxygen, fostering microorganisms that break down organic matter in wastewater, making them ideal for digesting high-strength effluents. In contrast, aerobic rotating biological contactors utilize oxygen-rich environments to promote aerobic bacteria, efficiently treating lower-strength wastewater while facilitating enhanced oxygen transfer. Both technologies offer effective solutions for wastewater management, catering to different treatment needs and operational requirements.</p></p>
